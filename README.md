# Sales-Data-Analysis-Dashboard-using-Excel
This project analyzes retail sales data to identify key revenue trends and product performance.  An interactive dashboard was built using Excel Pivot Tables, slicers, and charts to visualize sales metrics such as revenue by region, product category, payment methods, and monthly sales trends.

**Dashboard Features**

• Revenue analysis by region and product category

• Payment method distribution visualization

• Monthly sales trend analysis

• Top-performing product identification

• Interactive filters using slicers

**Key Insights**

• North America generated the highest revenue among all regions.

• Electronics category contributed the largest share of total revenue.

• Credit card payments represented the majority of transactions.

• A small number of products contribute significantly to overall revenue.
